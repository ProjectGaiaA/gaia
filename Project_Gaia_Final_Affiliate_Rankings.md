## DECISIVE FACTOR: Espresso Dominance

Espresso isn't just #1 — it's in a different league:

- **Revenue/sale is 3-10x every other niche**
- **Zero competition** (only GREEN niche with high AOV)
- **6+ specialty retailers** all with affiliate programs
- **Universal taxonomy** (model numbers identical everywhere)
- **Year-round demand** (no seasonality)
- **Passionate community** (r/espresso 500K+)
- **90-day cookies** at Clive and My Espresso Shop

The gap between espresso and #2 is massive. Aquarium equipment is a solid backup but BRS's 7-day cookie hurts, and the community is much smaller.

Garden plants (the original Project Gaia concept) ranks #3 but revenue per sale is 7-10x lower than espresso. You'd need 7-10x the traffic to match espresso revenue.
