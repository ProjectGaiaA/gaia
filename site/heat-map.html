Category Summary

Azaleas Rhododendrons

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: March-April

Encore Azaleas sell out early in spring. Buy in fall for best selection AND best price.

Blueberries

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: March-April

Buy 2-3 varieties for cross-pollination. Spring planting gives the best first-year fruit production.

Flowering Trees

✓ Best buy: October-November

⚠ Most expensive: March-April

If a specific cultivar matters, buy in March before sellout. If price matters more, wait for fall.

Fruit Trees

✓ Best buy: October-November

⚠ Most expensive: March

Order bare-root trees in January for best selection. If buying potted, wait for fall clearance.

Grasses

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: April-May

Buy in fall, plant in fall. Fall-planted grasses establish stronger root systems over winter and fill out faster the following spring.

Groundcovers

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: April-May

Buy in fall, plant in fall. Fall-planted groundcovers establish stronger root systems over winter and spread faster the following spring.

Houseplants

✓ Best buy: Year-round

⚠ Most expensive: None

Shop big-box stores for common sizes. Specialty nurseries charge more but offer larger, more established specimens.

Hydrangeas

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: April-May

Buy in fall, plant in fall. Fall-planted hydrangeas establish stronger root systems over winter and bloom the following spring.

Japanese Maples

✓ Best buy: October-November

⚠ Most expensive: March-April

Fall planting gives roots 4-5 months to establish before summer stress. Best value is bare-root in February.

Perennials

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: April-May

Buy in fall, plant in fall. Fall-planted perennials establish stronger root systems over winter and bloom the following spring.

Privacy Trees

✓ Best buy: October-November

⚠ Most expensive: April

Fall is actually ideal for planting evergreens. Buy in October for best price AND best planting conditions.

Roses

✓ Best buy: September-October

⚠ Most expensive: March-April

For Knock Out roses, fall planting works well in zones 6-9. Bare-root in late winter is cheapest.

Shade Trees

✓ Best buy: October-November

⚠ Most expensive: March

Order bare-root trees in January for best selection. If buying potted, wait for fall clearance.
