Dwarf Alberta Spruce has a distinct December price bump because landscapers use potted specimens as living Christmas trees. Spring is the main buying season when containers are freshly stocked. Heat-sensitive - not recommended south of zone 6.

Pro tip: Buy in fall for the best deals and ideal planting weather. Avoid hot, dry sites - this conifer dislikes summer heat above zone 6. Watch for spider mites in warm climates.
