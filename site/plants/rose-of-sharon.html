Rose of Sharon is sold under many cultivar names - Lucy, Pink Chiffon, Blue Bird, Azurri Blue Satin, Lavender. Prices vary widely by cultivar, with newer patented varieties commanding premium pricing. Fall clearance offers 15-30% savings.

Pro tip: Buy in fall for clearance. Standard cultivars (Lucy, Blue Bird) are cheaper than newer patented series like Chiffon or Satin. All bloom prolifically in late summer when few other shrubs do.
