Red Sunset is widely considered the best Red Maple cultivar for fall color reliability. Often priced slightly higher than October Glory due to demand. Fall clearance is the best time to buy.

Pro tip: Buy in fall when inventory is clearing. Red Sunset colors up earlier than most Red Maples, so you get a longer fall display.
